# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Israeli SMBs and growing businesses who are stuck doing repetitive work manually. They know they need automation and custom tooling, but lack the internal engineering team to build it. They arrive skeptical of tech agencies and need to see credibility fast. They care about results, not jargon.

## Product Purpose

Altro AI is a boutique Israeli AI agency that builds custom web apps and workflow automations for businesses. It exists to close the gap between enterprise-grade tooling and small business reality. Success means a visitor books a call because they trust the agency's craft and believe it can solve their specific problem.

## Brand Personality

Premium. Crafted. Calm. — the confident silence of a studio that doesn't need to shout. Like Stripe or Linear but warmer, more human. Intelligence expressed through restraint: clean systems, purposeful motion, zero filler.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS (Hubspot, Salesforce) — blue corporate, template-feel, no identity
- AI startup clichés (2024 wave) — purple-to-blue gradient text, floating brains, "supercharge" copy, robot imagery
- Awwwards scroll-jacking — effects over clarity, tech for its own sake
- Cream/warm minimal (Notion, Linear adjacent) — low energy for an agency that moves fast

## Design Principles

1. **Show precision, don't claim it.** Every detail that's tight signals craft. Padding inconsistencies, font-weight drift, misaligned grids — these say "rushed." The design IS the pitch.
2. **Motion earns its place.** Nothing moves without a reason. Ambient animation (hero wave mesh) creates aliveness; interactive motion (reveals, hovers) provides feedback. Neither competes with content.
3. **Dark is deliberate.** The dark navy palette isn't "because tools look dark." It signals depth, focus, and premium — appropriate for infrastructure that runs invisibly in the background.
4. **Teal as a signal, not wallpaper.** The brand color marks things worth noticing: CTAs, accents, the energy of the system. Overusing it kills its meaning.
5. **Israeli directness.** The voice is confident and specific. No filler phrases. Sentences say what they mean.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. All animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — show static frames or instant transitions. Bilingual (Hebrew/English) — the site has i18n in place. Contrast ratios enforced: body text ≥4.5:1, large headings ≥3:1.
